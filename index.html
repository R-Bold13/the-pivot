personal creative journal of Ray Bolden documenting a practice still being built.

A personal commitment to creating in public, trusting a voice years in the making, and an unorthodox path now understood as a strength. The Pivot is an empirical way of thinking, shaped as much by curiosity and humility as by repetition, sustained attention, and lived experience.

Created not to perform or demonstrate awareness of what is current, but to explore restraint as expression and the ways emotional resonance, identity, and technical dimensions shape one another.

why the pivot?

The Pivot is a personal acknowledgment of someone who spent years knowing how to think before knowing how to say it. A record of an empirical practice rooted in transdisciplinary thinking, built on the belief that perspective is worth sharing and that truth and guidance can be found beyond the boundaries of any single discipline. Shared not because it is finished, but because it is honest.

Above all, this space exists to document finding my voice and a perspective I now know is worth sharing.
